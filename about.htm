All About the Kitchen!

Attila's Kitchen is the online cookbook for everyone! From humble beginnings, one idea for a website project by five college freshman transformed into the hit cookbook phenomenon sweeping the nation! With the simple notion of gathering recipes together into one website, success soon flourished outward. Great idea right? Well, you can't expect any less from the School of Innovation, Stevens Institute of Technology. Fun Fact: the name Attila even derived from the school's mascot, talk about school pride!
